Lionel Jin

Hi! I'm Lionel, a Business Analyst at McKinsey passionate about the intersection of healthcare and tech.

Before McKinsey, I productized Smart City SaaS offerings on JD.com's AI platform team, drove the development of the Azure DevOps mobile app at Microsoft and worked on computational drug discovery at D. E. Shaw Research.

I grew up in Singapore where I was commissioned as an infantry officer and served as Lieutenant in the Air Force.
